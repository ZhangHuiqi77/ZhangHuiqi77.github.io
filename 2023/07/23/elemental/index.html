Elemental

Posted on 2023-07-23 In Life

‘Elemental’ is a great Disney movie. It tells a story about Be Yourself. The story happens in the world of elements, fire, water, gas, wood. The main charactor is a fire girl Ember. She fall in love with a water boy, Wade. They together save Ember’s father’s shop, during which Ember find she loves Wade and she learned how to express herself from him. For Wade, he was attracted and moved by Ember’s enthusiasm and confidence. They together make impossible to be possible. Usually fire and water are opposite, conflict and fight each other. Ember and Wade show big tension and how they can actually cooperate.

Except the central topic, these points also hit me:

  1. For ember’s parents, they know immigration would be hard and they were even not supported by their family and friends, but they still choose this. Because there exist unchangeable bad things in their hometown. They want to have a better life. Immigration is also for Ember. I feel that it is very similar in real life. Every country has its system defect. If the defect is not appropriate for a person, then the person may think about change, which usually need to face a great difficulty, like language, life style, acceptance. Also, in the immigration country, the local people also need to face some challenge from the immigrants. The bias come from their difference. The movie shows the inconvience of two so different people and also show amazing part.

  2. The movie show many things that I think are like traditional Chinese stuff. Like the ceremony when Ember leave, the bow down to the ground. And a specific stuff means the continue of a family and career, like the blue lantern for Ember’s family. I actually don’t like these part in Chinese culture. But it shows in the Disney movie means that people around the world can understand these feelings.

  3. When Ember and Wade are traped in the glass room and guard the blue lantern. Wade choose to sacrificed himself, be evaporated. Eventually he comes back! Water is stronger than what they look like. The similar thing for water and fire is they both are flexible and can be in any shape. But water also can change their state! Even if evaporated, they are still themself!

  4. Be true self is not easy. A more common reason is that many voice comes and boo you, tell you you can not make it. So most people become what other people think about them. People all know growing up in the underrated condition is hard. But sometimes, it may even harder to grow up in the high expectations from people who love you. Because you care about them and want them be happy. So a smart way is to always be independent of other people’s thinking, always leave a space for youself. In addition, real love is to support, not to expect.
